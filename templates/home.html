What is OpenClinical? Show me more

The OpenClinical approach

OpenClinical.net is taking the next radical step, by providing tools and techniques to empower you and your organisation to share knowledge of best practice in specialist fields, create and publish applications, trial them at the point-of-care and translate new research into routine services.

Expert knowledge

Formalised best practice medical knowledge, provided by clinical experts.

Open publishing

Machine-executable content, published open source and open access.

Up to date

Open discussion and feedback to develop up-to-the-minute consensus.

Improving care

Access and use in-depth care guidance to inform your medical knowledge and processes.
